What makes a password secure?

Long

The longer the password, the more secure it is. Secure password should contain at least 10 characters

Complex

Strong passwords use a combination of letters, numbers, letters and symbols to create a string of unpredictable characters, characters that are not like words or words.

Unical

A strong password should be unique for each account to reduce vulnerability in the event of a hack.
